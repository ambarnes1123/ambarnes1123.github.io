Therapy Groups

Child Therapy Services

Child therapy services are delivered through a variety of methods. We currently offer individual and play therapy services to children and adolescents.

Fitness Classes

We provide motivation, nutrition, and fitness guidance for youth and adults.

Yoga

Yoga and mindfulness training can help to alleviate symptoms of anxiety and depression, enhance sleep quality, and improve self-esteem. Children will learn how to bring awareness to their bodies through mindfulness techniques and how to calm their minds through breathing exercises and yoga.

Nutrition Guidance

Kids and families learn to create healthy meals, how to make better choices when dining out and maintaining nutrition.
