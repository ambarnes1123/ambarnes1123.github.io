Meet The Team

Our team is passionate about helping children and adolescents become the best versions of themselves and carry out their future goals. We have a talented team of professionals who all bring unique experiences and talents.

Christine Willing

Founder & Partner

More Information

Mrs. Willing has worked as a school psychologist in Northern Virginia for the past 8 years, before opening up her own practice. Her extensive knowledge and experience includes working with special education laws and procedures, comprehensive psychological evaluations, developing IEP's, behavioral consulting, and implementing accommodations through a 504 plan. Throughout her career, Mrs. Willing has completed hundreds of psychological evaluations on school age students, pre-k through high school. Her comprehensive evaluations contribute to the development of academic, social, and emotional supports for students. She has also provided individual and group counseling to students with a wide variety of needs. Including, but not limited to, ADHD/executive functioning, social skill development, behavior management, and anxiety/depressive disorders. Mrs. Willing received her undergraduate degree in Psychology from James Madison University and graduate degree in School Psychology from the University of North Carolina, at Chapel-Hill. During her graduate studies she worked at the Frank Porter Graham Child Developmental Institute in Chapel Hill as a research assistant on the Carolina Neurodevelopmental Disabilities Project. There Mrs. Willing studied the language development in children with Autism, Fragile X Syndrome, and Down syndrome. She has also worked at the Duke University ADHD Clinic, where she supervised and administered psychological testing to participants. Mrs. Willing is currently a Licensed School Psychologist. Mrs. Willing has provided extensive therapeutic services to children, adolescents, and young adults suffering from severe anxiety, depression, and executive functioning deficits. Mrs. Willing works to design and implement highly specific positive behavior support plans to reduce inappropriate behavior while enhancing adaptive skills to replace problematic behavior. Mrs. Willing educates and trains the family and others who live and work with the individual to set up a consistent system of support across all settings. Mrs. Willing is passionate about creating a positive lifestyle in order to improve mental and physical health. She currently resides in DC with her husband.

Joe Avey

Fitness Expert

More Information

Joe Avey has been in the fitness industry for two years as Group Exercise instructor and Personal Trainer. During this time he has coached over 2,000 classes at Orange Theory Clarendon, Rosslyn, Ballston and Navy Yard. As the head coach at OrangeTheory Clarendon he has been instrumental in building one of the top performing studios in a franchise that now has over 1,000 locations worldwide . Over the course of these two years, he has seen firsthand how the success of any individual's fitness journey is directly tied to the trust they build with their coach. The perfect program is only as effective as the amount of trust the individual has with their instructor, trainer or coach. Joe has worked extremely hard to maximize the trust with the members at Orangetheory by constantly providing passion for the brand and a burning desire to help others achieve their goals. Joe is now bringing the same passion, enthusiasm and desire to Think Happy, Live Healthy with the intention of serving as a mentor to the teens in our program and guide them along their journey. He will use fitness as a way to create habits that naturally build self discipline and confidence.

Joshua Rasch

Fitness Expert

More Information

Joshua Rasch has been physically coaching and mentally training youth for over ten years. He started out working with summer camps focused on encouraging teenagers to face their mental roadblocks and grow through controlled, challenging experiences. Realizing a passion for youth through this experience, he began to seek out unique opportunities to marry challenging, physical experiences with mindset growth and development. This vision quickly led to the opportunity to train and lead groups of youth to numerous countries including Botswana, Thailand, Panama, Costa Rica, and Mexico to do humanitarian and service work. He continued to build on these experiences back in the States by speaking to high-schoolers in the public school system on the self-esteem and relational challenges affecting so many teenagers. Currently Joshua splits his time between coaching students and adults, always carrying a strong focus on mental health and a strong mindset. Joshua lives in Washington DC and is active and outdoors as much as possible. He is a firm believer in the power of mindset training coupled with physical activity, and can personally speak to its transformative power.
